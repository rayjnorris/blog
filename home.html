Author:

The leopard (Panthera pardus) /ˈlɛpərd/ is one of the five "big cats" in the genus Panthera. It is a member of the family Felidae with a wide range in sub-Saharan Africa and parts of Asia.  Fossil records suggest that in the Late Pleistocene it occurred in Europe and Japan.
